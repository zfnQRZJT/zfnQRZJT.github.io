What is this site?

It's an encyclopedia of many Minecraft prisons. Seeing Pandora's Vault on the Dream SMP, many Minecraft players have rushed to build prisons that fix the clear flaws that Pandora's Vault had, which would non-canonically allow its prisoner, Dream, to escape.

As you might expect, a community of escapists has also cropped up, trying to find the cracks in each prison's armor, escaping from the inside or breaking the prisoner out.
